Our Story

Rooted in Florala

Alabama Land & Home opened its doors in 2006, and ever since, owner Glen Powell and our team have helped neighbors across South Alabama and the Florida Panhandle find the right property — whether that's a quiet country home, a family farm or a few acres to hunt and roam.

Florala sits right on the Alabama–Florida line, and so do we: we know both sides of the line, the back roads, the land, and what makes this part of the South special. That local knowledge is the heart of what we do.

We believe in honest advice, fair dealing and treating you the way we'd want to be treated — like a neighbor, not a transaction.
